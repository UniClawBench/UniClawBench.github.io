Senior Backend Engineer — Distributed Systems

We are hiring a Senior Backend Engineer to own the checkout and payments
pipeline. You will lead the migration of a monolith to a modular service
fleet on Kubernetes, tune our Postgres and Redis layer, and design
idempotent event-driven flows on Kafka.

Must-have:
- 6+ years of production backend experience with Go or Python
- Deep familiarity with PostgreSQL (schema design, tuning, online migration)
- Kafka / Pulsar or equivalent event stream system
- Service-level reliability instincts (SLOs, error budgets)
- Hands-on with Kubernetes at scale (deployments, horizontal pod autoscaling)

Nice to have:
- OpenTelemetry, eBPF, Rust
- Prior checkout / payments work

We prioritize candidates who can show measurable impact: reduced latency,
eliminated toil, increased SLO attainment, grew throughput.
