## Directors and Executive Officers

Our Board of Directors (our Board) is composed of highly experienced and
diverse directors who have led, advised, and established leading global
organizations and institutions. Biographical information of our directors
and executive officers as of April 9, 2024 is set forth below.

### Directors

**Larry Page** — Co-Founder. Director since 1998 | Executive Committee (Chair).
Larry Page, 51, one of Google's Co-Founders, previously served as Google's
Chief Executive Officer from April 2011 to October 2015, and as Alphabet's
Chief Executive Officer from October 2015 to December 2019.

**Sergey Brin** — Co-Founder. Director since 1998 | Executive Committee.
Sergey Brin, 50, one of Google's Co-Founders, previously served as Google's
President from May 2011 to October 2015, and as Alphabet's President from
October 2015 to December 2019.

**Sundar Pichai** — Chief Executive Officer, Alphabet and Google.
Director since 2017 | Executive Committee. Sundar Pichai, 51, joined Google
in 2004 and was named the Chief Executive Officer of Google in October
2015 and of Alphabet in December 2019. Sundar has led product and
engineering for Google's products and platforms, including Search, Chrome,
Maps, Android, Gmail, and Google Workspace.

**John L. Hennessy** — Chair of the Board. Independent Director since 2004 |
Nominating and Corporate Governance Committee (Chair). John L. Hennessy,
71, has served as Chair of our Board since January 2018. John previously
served as our Lead Independent Director from April 2007 to January 2018.
John is the James F. and Mary Lynn Gibbons Professor of Computer Science
and Electrical Engineering in the Stanford School of Engineering, and
served as President of Stanford University from September 2000 to August
2016.

**Frances H. Arnold** — Independent Director since 2019 | Nominating and
Corporate Governance Committee. Frances H. Arnold, 67, is the Linus
Pauling Professor of Chemical Engineering, Bioengineering and
Biochemistry at the California Institute of Technology, and a Nobel
Laureate in Chemistry.

**R. Martin "Marty" Chávez** — Independent Director since 2022 | Audit
and Compliance Committee. R. Martin "Marty" Chávez, 60, has been a
Partner and Vice Chairman of Sixth Street, a global asset manager, since
May 2021. From January 2005 until his retirement in December 2019, he
served in a number of executive positions at Goldman Sachs, including
Chief Information Officer, Chief Financial Officer, and global co-head of
the firm's Securities Division.

**L. John Doerr** — Independent Director since 1999 | Leadership
Development, Inclusion and Compensation Committee. L. John Doerr, 72, has
been a General Partner of Kleiner Perkins, a venture capital firm, since
August 1980.

**Roger W. Ferguson Jr.** — Independent Director since 2016 | Audit and
Compliance Committee (Chair). Roger W. Ferguson Jr., 72, has been the
Chief Investment Officer of Red Cell Partners LLC since August 2022.
Roger has served as the President and Chief Executive Officer of TIAA
from April 2008 to May 2021. Roger was appointed as the Chair of the
Audit Committee effective October 31, 2023.

**K. Ram Shriram** — Independent Director since 1998 | Leadership
Development, Inclusion and Compensation Committee. K. Ram Shriram, 67,
has been a managing partner of Sherpalo Ventures, LLC, an angel venture
investment company, since January 2000.

**Robin L. Washington** — Independent Director since 2019 | Leadership
Development, Inclusion and Compensation Committee (Chair); Audit and
Compliance Committee. Robin L. Washington, 61, served as the Executive
Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of Gilead Sciences, Inc. from
May 2008 until her retirement in November 2019. Robin was appointed as a
member of the Audit Committee effective October 31, 2023.

**Ann Mather** — Independent Director since 2005 | Audit and Compliance
Committee. Ann Mather, 68, served as Executive Vice President and Chief
Financial Officer of Pixar Animation Studios from September 1999 to May
2004, and is a current or former director of public companies including
Netflix, Glassdoor, Shutterfly, MGM Holdings, and Bumble. Ann was
designated by our Board as the Audit Committee's "audit committee
financial expert" within the meaning of SEC rules.

**Sundeep Reddy** — Independent Director since 2023 | Nominating and
Corporate Governance Committee. Sundeep Reddy, 56, has been Managing
Partner of Continental Capital Advisors since June 2018, and previously
served as Senior Vice President of Global Treasury Operations at a
Fortune 50 issuer from January 2010 to May 2018. Sundeep was appointed
to the Board effective November 15, 2023.

**Yolanda Park** — Independent Director since 2023 | Leadership
Development, Inclusion and Compensation Committee. Yolanda Park, 58,
has served as Chief People Officer of MerakiHealth Inc. since March
2020 and previously held senior human-capital roles at three S&P 500
issuers between 2008 and 2020. Yolanda was appointed to the Board
effective December 4, 2023.

**Diego Salazar** — Independent Director since 2024 | Audit and
Compliance Committee. Diego Salazar, 54, is a former Vice Chair of
Banco Santander's investment-banking division (2016–2023) and has been
Chair of the financial-services nonprofit Capital for Good since
February 2023. Diego was appointed to the Board effective February 12,
2024 and joins the Audit Committee in connection with the seat
recently vacated by Mr. Bourgeois (see "Recently-Departed Directors"
below).

### Executive Officers

This section describes the business experience of our executive officers,
other than Sundar, whose biography appears above. Our executive officers
are appointed by and serve at the discretion of our Board.

**Ruth M. Porat** — President and Chief Investment Officer; Chief
Financial Officer, Alphabet and Google. Ruth M. Porat, 66, has served as
President and Chief Investment Officer of Alphabet and Google since
September 2023 and as Senior Vice President, Chief Financial Officer of
Google since May 2015 and has held the same title at Alphabet since it
was created in October 2015.

**Prabhakar Raghavan** — Senior Vice President, Knowledge and
Information, Google. Prabhakar Raghavan, 63, has served as Senior Vice
President of Google since November 2018. He is responsible for Google
Search, Assistant, Geo, Ads, Commerce, and Payments products.

**Philipp Schindler** — Senior Vice President, Chief Business Officer,
Google. Philipp Schindler, 53, has served as Senior Vice President, Chief
Business Officer of Google since August 2015, overseeing Google's and
YouTube's sales activities, Google's technical and consumer support,
partnership and business development teams, and country operations.

**Kent Walker** — President, Global Affairs, Chief Legal Officer and
Secretary, Alphabet and Google. Kent Walker, 63, has served as President,
Global Affairs, and Chief Legal Officer of Alphabet and Google since
November 2021, and Secretary of Alphabet since January 2020.

**Anat Ashkenazi** — Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer,
Google Cloud. Anat Ashkenazi, 51, has served as Senior Vice President of
Google Cloud since March 2024 and previously served as Chief Financial
Officer of Eli Lilly's Innovation Group. Ms. Ashkenazi reports into the
Office of the CEO via Ruth Porat for capital-allocation matters and is
included in our Section 16 officer roster.

**Hiroshi Ito** — Senior Vice President, People Operations, Alphabet
and Google. Hiroshi Ito, 49, has served in this role since January 2024.
He oversees compensation, talent acquisition, organizational design, and
employee experience for the combined Alphabet/Google entity and reports
through the Chief Executive Officer's office.

## Board Advisors and Observers

In addition to the directors listed above, our Board maintains a small
panel of non-voting advisors and observers who attend committee
meetings in an advisory capacity. They are not directors and do not
appear on the formal Board roster, but their advisory relationships are
disclosed below for transparency:

- **Eric E. Schmidt** — Technical Advisor to Alphabet's senior
  leadership team since June 2020 (following his departure from the
  Board in June 2019). Mr. Schmidt advises the Executive Committee on
  long-range strategic and technology-policy matters in a non-voting
  capacity. His advisory engagement is renewed annually.
- **Ann S. Bowers Liang** — Audit Committee non-voting observer since
  March 2024. Ms. Bowers Liang is a partner at the auditing-standards
  research firm Ledger Forum and attends Audit Committee sessions as a
  technical observer; she does not vote, sign committee resolutions,
  or count toward Audit Committee quorum.

## Board Leadership Structure

In January 2018, John L. Hennessy, the then Lead Independent Director,
was appointed to serve as Alphabet's Chair of the Board. In December
2019, Sundar Pichai became the Chief Executive Officer of Alphabet. Our
Board regularly reviews its leadership structure to ensure continued
effectiveness and believes that the current structure, which separates
the Chair and Chief Executive Officer roles, is appropriate at this time.

Each of the director nominees standing for election, other than Larry,
Sergey, and Sundar, is independent.

## Board Committees

Our Board has the following four standing committees:

1. an Audit and Compliance Committee (the Audit Committee),
2. a Leadership Development, Inclusion and Compensation Committee (the
   Compensation Committee),
3. a Nominating and Corporate Governance Committee (the Governance
   Committee), and
4. an Executive Committee.

### Audit and Compliance Committee

The main function of the Audit Committee is to oversee our accounting
and financial reporting processes, oversee our relationship with our
independent auditors, provide oversight regarding significant financial
matters, and review and discuss with management the company's major
risk exposures. During 2023, the Audit Committee held eight meetings
and acted by unanimous written/electronic consent three times.

The Audit Committee currently comprises Roger W. Ferguson Jr. (Chair),
Robin L. Washington, R. Martin "Marty" Chávez, Ann Mather, and Diego
Salazar, each of whom is a non-employee member of our Board. Ann S.
Bowers Liang attends Audit Committee meetings as a non-voting observer.

### Leadership Development, Inclusion and Compensation Committee

The purpose of the Compensation Committee is to oversee our leadership
development and compensation programs for the members of our Board and
our employees. During 2023, the Compensation Committee held five
meetings and acted by unanimous written/electronic consent eight times.

The Compensation Committee currently comprises Robin L. Washington
(Chair), L. John Doerr, K. Ram Shriram, and Yolanda Park, each of whom
is a non-employee member of our Board.

### Nominating and Corporate Governance Committee

The Governance Committee's purpose is to assist our Board in identifying
individuals qualified to become members of our Board, to oversee the
evaluation of the Board and management, and to develop and update our
corporate governance principles. During 2023, the Governance Committee
held four meetings.

The Governance Committee currently comprises John L. Hennessy (Chair),
Frances H. Arnold, and Sundeep Reddy, each of whom is a non-employee
member of our Board.

### Executive Committee

The Executive Committee serves as an administrative committee of our
Board to act upon and facilitate the consideration by senior management
and our Board of certain high-level business and strategic matters.
During 2023, the Executive Committee did not hold any meetings. The
Executive Committee currently comprises Larry Page (Chair), Sergey Brin,
and Sundar Pichai. Eric E. Schmidt attends Executive Committee meetings
as a non-voting Technical Advisor.
