**Location:** Lake Tahoe Lodge, Conf Room A

**Facilitator:** Priya Menon

## Day 1

### Opening session
We kicked off with each team member sharing one win from the prior quarter and one open
risk for Q3. Several themes emerged:

- The migration to the new analytics warehouse landed two weeks behind schedule but with
  zero data loss. We agreed this was a worthwhile trade-off.
- Hiring velocity slowed in August. Leila volunteered to revisit the JD copy and unblock
  the platform-engineer pipeline.
- Customer support tickets on the export feature are spiking. Need to investigate
  whether this is a usability issue or a real defect.

### Afternoon working session
Broke into three sub-groups around platform reliability, growth experiments, and
internal tooling. Each group produced a one-page summary that we'll consolidate into
the offsite report.

## Day 2

### Strategy review
Reviewed competitor positioning. Two new entrants in the SMB segment but neither is
showing meaningful adoption beyond their initial cohort. Decision: monitor monthly,
do not invest in counter-moves yet.

### Q3 commitments

2. Reduce p95 export latency from 14s to under 8s.
3. Hire two senior engineers and one designer.
4. Run three growth experiments on activation friction.
