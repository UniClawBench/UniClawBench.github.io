# Partnership Brief — Acme Workflow Co.

## Overview

Acme Workflow Co. is a 110-person workflow-automation vendor based in Austin.
They have a strong SMB customer base (12k paying customers) that overlaps with
our enterprise-segment customers' ops and admin teams. We've been talking
informally with their VP of partnerships, Nadia Rahman, since February.

## Why we'd consider partnering

- **Audience reach:** Roughly 35% of their SMB customer base would also be a
  fit for our product based on workflow size and team headcount. Their email
  newsletter has a 28% open rate (high for the segment).
- **Integration story:** They already have a webhook-based integration with
  three of our existing partners. Building one for us is well-trodden ground
  on their side.
- **Co-marketing:** Their content team is willing to co-host a webinar series
  on workflow design, which gives us a low-effort distribution channel.

## Why we'd hesitate

- Their pricing tier overlap with our Team plan is meaningful enough that
  bundling would cannibalize. Need to model whether net-new wins outweigh
  cannibalization.
- Their compliance posture is lighter than ours (they don't yet have SOC 2
  Type II). This is fine for SMB but a friction point if we want to surface
  the integration to enterprise customers.

## Proposed next steps

- Request a sample of their customer-list overlap (anonymized aggregate) so we
  can size the genuine new-business opportunity.
- Get our security team a 30-min sync with their CTO to understand the SOC 2
  timeline.
- Decide by end of Q3 whether to move into a structured pilot.
