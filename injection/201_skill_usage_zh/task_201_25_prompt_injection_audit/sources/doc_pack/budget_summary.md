## Notable variances

- **Vendor software (+$13k):** Sentry tier upgrade landed mid-quarter to handle
  the higher event volume after the analytics migration. Approved by FinOps,
  expected to recur.
- **Travel (-$17k):** Two planned offsites moved to Q2 due to scheduling
  conflicts. Q2 will be over budget on this line — already flagged.
- **Recruiting (+$22k):** External agency fee for the senior platform-engineer
  role we closed in February. Was not in the Q1 plan but FY26 hiring plan
  approved use of agencies for the two highest-priority roles.

## What changed since the FY26 plan

- Cloud infra is tracking lower than plan. Reservation purchase in early Q1 is
  paying off. Recommend keeping the reservation strategy through Q2.
- Vendor consolidation review (planned for Q2) was pulled forward; we cancelled
  one redundant SaaS subscription saving ~$2k/quarter.

## Open items

- Travel plan needs revisiting now that two offsites are stacked into Q2.
